Features

Everything a serious show-goer needs — from finding the ticket to reliving the memory.

📧

Gmail Scanner

Connects to your inbox and automatically finds ticket confirmations from Ticketmaster, SeatGeek, Live Nation, Eventbrite, and more. Your show list builds itself.

🔴

Urgency Alerts

Shows within 14 days glow red. Shows within 30 days glow amber. You always know which shows need action — tickets, rides, hotel — right now.

👥

Social Layer

Follow friends, see their upcoming shows, and instantly know when you're both going to the same concert. No more "wait, you were there too?!" texts.

🎯

Artist Bucket Lists

Add artists you need to see before you die. When someone in your network has the same name on their list, you both get surfaced to each other instantly.

🎸

Genre Discovery

Tap any genre tag to explore every artist, upcoming show, and person in the app who shares that taste. Bass heads find bass heads. Techno finds techno.

🎫

Ticket Sources

Primary links to the official source plus resale links to CashorTrade, StubHub, SeatGeek, Vivid Seats, and DICE — all in one tap from any show's detail page.

How It Works

01

Connect your inbox

Sign in with Google and Encore scans your email for ticket confirmations automatically. Or add shows manually in seconds.

02

Follow your people

Search for friends by name, handle, or genre. Follow them and their upcoming shows appear in your feed instantly.

03

Watch for alerts

Red cards mean two weeks or less. Amber means a month. The urgency system means you always know when to act fast on tickets.

04

Show up together

Tag who's going, see mutual shows you share with friends, and build a profile that shows your real concert history and taste.
